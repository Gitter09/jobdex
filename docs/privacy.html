Privacy Policy

We don't collect, store, or sell your data because we literally can't.

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. The Local-First Promise

JobDex is built around a single foundational architectural decision: it is entirely local-first. We do not have servers, databases, or analytics engines in the cloud designed to ingest your data. Everything happens exclusively on your machine.

2. Data Storage & Local Encryption

100% of your contact records, pipelines, notes, and email history are stored locally in an encrypted SQLCipher AES-256 database residing internally within your operating system's application support directory. Nobody but you has access to it. If you uninstall the app and securely delete your local database, your data is gone forever—we possess no backup copies.

3. Google Workspace APIs & Exhaustive Data Usage Disclosure

JobDex is a native desktop application that integrates directly with your Google or Microsoft email accounts to manage your job search outreach. To enable this, JobDex requests OAuth permission to read your email messages, create drafts, and send outgoing emails on your behalf.

Because JobDex operates completely offline, this data is NEVER transmitted to any external server. It is fetched strictly from the OAuth provider's API directly to your local machine and stored solely within your locally encrypted SQLite database.

Your Google Workspace and Microsoft OAuth access and refresh tokens are securely persisted utilizing AES-256-GCM encryption, managed natively by your OS's keychain wrapper (macOS Keychain or Windows Credential Manager). JobDex's creator, maintainers, and third-parties have precisely zero access to your credentials or email contents.

Google API Services User Data Policy (Limited Use Disclosure)

JobDex's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. JobDex strictly abides by this policy. We do not use your Google data for serving personalized or non-personalized advertisements, we do not allow humans to read your data (there is no "we" to read it—it resides exclusively on your personal device), and we do not transfer your data to any third-party AI models, brokers, or analytics services under any circumstances.

4. Tracking & Telemetry

We do not utilize tracking pixels to monitor our users. We do not run Google Analytics, Mixpanel, PostHog, or any user-behavior tracking engines within the app. JobDex does not "phone home" to report product usage metrics.

5. Contact & Support

If you have further questions or require clarifications regarding your privacy, you can reach out via our Contact Page.
